  1. Whisk 1cupflour,1tsp baking powder,1cupsugar, and pinch of salt together in a large bowl.
  2. Mix 1cup almond milk, 1/2cup yogurt, 2 egg, 1/2 lemon zest and juice, and 1tsp almond extract together in a separate bowl.
  3. stir in 1/2cup butter. Add to the flour mixture
  4. stir just until smooth and gently fold in blueberries.
  5. Preheat a lightly oiled griddle or large skillet over medium-high heat.
  6. For each pancake, pour approximately 1/4 cup of batter onto the hot griddle.
  7. Cook until the pancakes start to lightly bubble and edges begin to dry, 3 to 4 minutes.
  8. Flip and continue cooking until lightly browned and cooked through, 1 to 2 minutes.
